Facts about the Houses in Game of Thrones

Game of Thrones is one of my favorite shows, but it's very complex. I know I would have appreciated some kind of guide before starting to watch Game of Thrones.

Here's some information on the houses in Game of Thrones.

Houses in Game of Thrones

Some of the most important Game of Thrones Houses.

House Targaryen

House Targaryen was founded by Aegon the Dragon (even though he wasn't a dragon.) House Targaryen once ruled the seven kingdoms, but was overthrown by House Baratheon. The last female Targaryen, Daenerys, had three (now two) dragons that she calls her children. Daenerys is now trying to take back the Iron Throne from House Lannister

House Lannister

House Lannister is located in Westerland, but the majority of House Lannister is actaully in the capital of the Seven Kingdoms, King's Landing. King's Landing is in the Crownlands on the east coast of Westeros. Lann the Clever founded House Lannister.

House Stark

House Stark rules the north in Westeros, and was founded by Brandon the Builder. Brandon the Builder lived in the age of heroes, and a main character in the series now (Bran Stark) is named after him. Brandon the Builder is said to have raised the wall that protects the north.
